# If Google Map Crashes

If Google Maps crashes or isn't working as expected, don't worry. This guide will help you find alternative options and get back on track.

## Steps to Take if Google Maps Crashes

### Option 1
**Download Alternative Apps**:
   - For bus routes, download **Moovit**.

       </div>
       <img src="/img/arrow_forward.png" className={styles.arrow} alt="arrow" />
     </Link>
     After you moovit is installed on your phone, you are all set! ReentryGuide GR will automatically use Moovit as bus option.

   - For car and walking directions, download **Here WeGo**.
     <Link className={styles.iconButton} to="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.here.app.maps&hl=en_US&gl=US" style={{ backgroundColor: '#EAE0D4' }}>

### Option 2
**Use Google Maps Web**:
   - If nothing else works, you can use Google Maps Web through your browser. But you need to follow these steps: 
   1. Go to the settings menu.
   2. Find the option to disable Google Maps. 
   3. Uninstall Moovit and Here WeGo. 
   4. After you disabled google maps and other map applications, our app will use the browser.

Once Google Maps is disabled, the app will use the web version, which might be more stable and reliable.

## Need More Help?

If you still need help after trying these steps, please contact your navigator.
