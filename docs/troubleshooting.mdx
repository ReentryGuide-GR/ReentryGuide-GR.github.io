### Option 2
**Use Google Maps Web**:
   - If nothing else works, you can use "Google Maps Web" through your browser. But you need to follow these steps: 
   1. Go to the settings.
   <img src="/img/tutorial/1.png" alt="Mockup" title="Mockup" width="300" className="shadow-img" />
   2. Click on "Apps".
   <img src="/img/tutorial/2.png" alt="Mockup" title="Mockup" width="300" className="shadow-img" />
   3. If you see "Maps", click on that one, then go to step 5.  If you don't see "Maps", Click on "See all (number) apps". For example, click on "See all 63 apps"
   <img src="/img/tutorial/3.png" alt="Mockup" title="Mockup" width="300" className="shadow-img" />
   4. Scroll down, and find "Maps".
   <img src="/img/tutorial/5.png" alt="Mockup" title="Mockup" width="300" className="shadow-img" />
   5. Click on "Maps".
   <img src="/img/tutorial/6.png" alt="Mockup" title="Mockup" width="300" className="shadow-img" />
   6. Click "Disable"
   <img src="/img/tutorial/4.png" alt="Mockup" title="Mockup" width="300" className="shadow-img" />

   7. After you disabled google maps and other map applications, our app will use the browser.

Once Google Maps is disabled, the app will use the web version, which might be more stable and reliable.
